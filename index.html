About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Indian Social Institute (ISI), New Delhi. I hold an MPhil and a PhD in Regional Development (Geography) from Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), India.

My research focuses on family demography, migration, child marriage, gender-based violence, maternal and child health, school education, and gender issues in South Asia.

Recognition: Ranked among the world’s top 2% of scientists (Stanford University, 2023 & 2024).

Research Interests

  • Distress and circular migration
  • Brick kiln workers and informal labour
  • Child marriage and child labour
  • Gender-based violence
  • Maternal and child health
  • Education and school participation
